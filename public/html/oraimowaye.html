Challenge

oraimo , being Africa’s No 1 Smart Accessories brand needed a way to build better connection with its target audience,being young millennials and Gen-Zs. How do we show them we understand? How do we turn them from mere buyers, to loyalists and core brand ambassadors?

the magic

Despite being very self-reliant, they still trust the opinions of real people, this has fuelled the era of influencers and content creators. We reached out to the TG, and using their recommendations, shortlisted unique personalities which they identified as influential in each space.

The “What Are You Exploring” campaign was a 10-episode series focused on getting these influencers to tell their stories, and encourage our target audience to keep pushing despite the hurdles they face, assuring them that their dreams are valid. We were deliberate about ensuring the content was created in a relax vibe, not focused directly on selling products, but on building brand love and trust.

the result

Rising consumer perception dnd Increased positive sentiments Over 25,000,000 impressions with increased brand talkability
