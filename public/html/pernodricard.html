Challenge

Pernod Ricard, World global leader in wine and spirits was looking to find how do we inspire change, creative and elevate consumer experiences in Nigeria going into 2022? This was the challenge presented to Echo Africa team to “crack.’”

the magic

Deployed a trend safari team to the street, to capture culture - music, food, experiences. Also, conducted triads with Genz’s and Millenials living in key cities who are night lifers. The study unearthed a number of insights that inspired experience overhaul and product development.

Research approach

Trend safari across lagos and abuja, 6 triads (FGDs), one one interviews, and desktop culture research.

the result

First ever Nigeria focused trend report for PR

Revamp of TA’S for PR’s portfolio brands

Launch of new sku;s and consumer experience platforms
