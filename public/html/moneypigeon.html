Challenge

How do we position an American start-up as a challenger brand within the fintech space? How do we give the brand presence and what communication levers do we use to sell its product offering to the right audience.

the magic

Through research, we discovered that Nigerians are looking for more efficient ways of making cross-border payments and trust is a big issue within this space. Using this key insight, we came up with a brand and content framework which we then used to develop its website and communication materials online & offline. Our approach is to maintain a relevant, valuable and relatable sweet spot between what the consumer want to engage with and what we must push out as brand communication.

the result

App optimizations & UI Experience
